## Open Data portals

- [Sveriges dataportal](https://www.dataportal.se/) - National open data portal aggregating datasets from Swedish authorities
- [Statistics Sweden (SCB)](https://www.scb.se/en/services/open-data-api/) - Official population, economic, and demographic statistics with open APIs
- [Sveriges Riksbank Open Data](https://www.riksbank.se/en-gb/statistics/) - Central bank financial statistics, exchange rates, and economic indicators
- [Swedish Courts (Domstolsverket)](https://www.domstol.se/) - Court information and public judgments under the principle of public access

## Legal Entities

- [Bolagsverket](https://www.bolagsverket.se/) - Official register of Swedish companies, associations, and beneficial ownership
- [Allabolag](https://www.allabolag.se/) - Company financials, board members, and corporate structure
- [Proff.se](https://www.proff.se/) - Business intelligence on Swedish companies and executives
- [UC Företagsinfo](https://www.uc.se/) - Credit and company information (some data behind paywalls)

## Maps

- [Lantmäteriet](https://www.lantmateriet.se/) - National mapping authority with cadastral maps and geodata
- [Geodata.se](https://www.geodata.se/) - Portal for Swedish geographic and spatial datasets
- [OpenStreetMap Sweden](https://www.openstreetmap.org/) - Community-driven open map data
- [SCB Open Geodata](https://www.scb.se/en/services/open-data-api/open-geodata/) - Administrative boundaries and geographic statistics

## Vehicles

- [Transportstyrelsen – Vehicle Register](https://www.transportstyrelsen.se/) - Official vehicle registration authority
- [Biluppgifter.se](https://www.biluppgifter.se/) - Vehicle information by license plate
- [Car.info](https://www.car.info/) - Vehicle specifications and registration history

## People
#### Some resources listed in this section contain personal data. Users must ensure compliance with GDPR, including lawful purpose, data minimization, and avoiding systematic profiling or redistribution.

- [Ratsit](https://www.ratsit.se/) - Names, addresses, age, and household information
- [Hitta.se](https://www.hitta.se/) - Address lookup, phone numbers, and maps
- [Eniro](https://www.eniro.se/) - Swedish yellow and white pages
- [MrKoll](https://mrkoll.se/) - Public records aggregation
- Try [OSINT Industries](https://app.osint.industries) and don't forget about the [Digital Profiler's Toolkit](https://github.com/paulpogoda/Digital-Profiler-s-Toolkit)

## Public procurement

- [Upphandlingsmyndigheten](https://www.upphandlingsmyndigheten.se/) - National authority for public procurement
- [Opic](https://www.opic.com/) - Public procurement notices and tender intelligence
- [e-Avrop](https://www.e-avrop.com/) - Electronic procurement platform
- [Visma Opic](https://www.visma.se/opic/) - Procurement monitoring and historical tenders
